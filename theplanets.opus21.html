The Planets (Op. 21)

Arrangement for Solo Piano


Year of Composition: 2023
Duration: 50 minutes
Premiere: December 5th 2023 ; Recital Room, The Purcell School


Composer's Notes:

The first full arrangement of Holst "The Planets" in history. Additionally, on a personal note, in the process of arranging, Holst's massive work really began to impact me over time. Since I've got to know it so well, it has began to impact most of my compositional work in some way.
