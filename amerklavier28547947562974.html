About the Festival

The Classical Music Festival is an annual celebration of timeless compositions and world-class performances, bringing together music lovers from around the globe for an unforgettable season of sound. Each year, the festival offers a blend of tradition and innovation, showcasing the finest orchestras, soloists, and emerging talents.

About the Founder

Maestro Jane Doe founded the Classical Music Festival in 2001 with a vision to bring timeless symphonies and brilliant performers to the heart of the city. With a background in conducting and education, Jane has led the festival to international acclaim.
