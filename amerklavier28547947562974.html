About the Founder

Maestro Jane Doe founded the Classical Music Festival in 2001 with a vision to bring timeless symphonies and brilliant performers to the heart of the city. With a background in conducting and education, Jane has led the festival to international acclaim.

This Year's Season

Join us for the 2025 season featuring performances from world-renowned orchestras, chamber ensembles, and soloists. Highlights include:

  • Opening Gala: Beethoven's 9th Symphony
  • Chamber Night: Works by Mozart, Debussy, and Clara Schumann
  • Rising Stars Showcase featuring young international talent

Past Highlights

Over the years, the festival has hosted unforgettable performances, including:

  • Yo-Yo Ma’s solo cello recital (2018)
  • Vienna Philharmonic’s guest appearance (2021)
  • A multimedia performance of Vivaldi’s Four Seasons with immersive visuals (2023)
