Empire of the Sun (Op. 30)

Arrangement for Violin and Piano


Year of Composition: 2024
Duration: 4 minutes
Premiere: TO BE ANNOUNCED


Composer's Notes:

This arrangement is from one of my favourite films of all time, Empire of the Sun, which I was introduced to by a friend of mine about a year ago. The film is fantastic, and the John Williams score is equally as fantastic, one of his best, in my opinion, so naturally I had to arrange it!
