Two Shakespeare Songs (Op. 13)

for S.A.T.B. Choir


Year of Composition: 2022
Duration: 7 minutes
Premiere: November 2022 ; Constance Pilkington Hall, The Purcell School


Composer's Notes:

Simple settings of two deep and beatiful Shakespeare poems, including "Full Fathom Five" from The Tempest, and "It was a lover and his lass" from As You Like It.
