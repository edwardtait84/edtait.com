Fleeting Manhattan (Op. 28)

for Orchestra


Year of Composition: 2023
Duration: 4 minutes
Premiere: February 12th 2024 ; St. John's Smith Square, London


Composer's Notes:

I was inspired to compose Fleeting Manhattan during my visit to New York City in October 2023. In particular, I was struck by the dynamic contrast between the lively and thrilling streets of Manhattan, and the stunning tranquility of the city's green spaces. The resulting experience formed the basis for my composition. It is one of fantasy, like an uncanny piece of theatre, but at the same time, reflects something absolutely beautiful!
