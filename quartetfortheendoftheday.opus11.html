Quartet for the End of the Day (Op. 11)

for Clarinet Quartet (Clarinet, Violin, Cello and Piano)


Year of Composition: 2022
Duration: 4 minutes
Premiere: March 2022 ; Recital Room, The Purcell School (featuring Joy Farrall, Charles Stewart, Nicholas Jenkinson and Debbie Shaw)


Composer's Notes:

This is a short Quartet Interlude, for the same instrumentation as Messiaen's "Quartet for the End of Time". Similarly, this piece is based on the use of isorhythms, inspired by a Purcell School seminar with composer Robert Saxton.
