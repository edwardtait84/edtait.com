Biography

Katrina Keyek is an American composer and saxophonist. She was born in 2006 in Florida, however she spent most of her life in Austin, Texas. At the age of 10, Katrina started to learn the saxophone and she was an established member in multiple wind ensembles and saxophone quartets, making Region and Area state bands a few times.
