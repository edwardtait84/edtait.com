Experiment on Light Refraction

April 10, 2026

This experiment explores how light bends when passing through different materials. Initial tests were conducted using glass and water.

The results showed a measurable change in angle depending on the density of the medium. Further measurements are required for accuracy.

Future work will include testing additional materials and comparing results with theoretical predictions.

Field Notes: Botanical Survey

April 8, 2026

A survey of local plant species was conducted in a woodland area. Several previously unrecorded specimens were identified.

Environmental conditions were mild, with moderate humidity and temperature.

Next steps include cataloguing specimens and cross-referencing with existing databases.
