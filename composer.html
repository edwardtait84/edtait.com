Biography

Edward Tait is a British composer whose work explores lyricism, colour, and dramatic narrative through contemporary classical music. His compositions have been performed by chamber ensembles, soloists, and orchestras across the United Kingdom and Europe.

Recent projects have focused on interdisciplinary collaboration, combining music with theatre, poetry, and visual media. His work is influenced by landscape, literature, and modern harmonic language, while remaining rooted in expressive melodic writing.
